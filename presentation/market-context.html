Problem

Only operators who can source roughly 500k HYPE can deploy a HIP-3 perp DEX. That is a major capital blocker before oracle, data, market-maker, audit, and legal costs.

Fee gap

Trade.xyz proves demand, but standard deployer economics accrue to the operator fee recipient. HYPE holders do not automatically share the operator upside.

HIP.markets answer

Community-funded HYPE backs the operator stake. HIP.markets operates curated markets and shares net deployer fees with the stakers who made those markets possible.
