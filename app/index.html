Community-backed market operator

HIP.markets uses deposited HYPE as the slashable stake for the HIP.markets team's own builder-deployed perpetual DEX on Hyperliquid. Stakers receive a share of net deployer fees while taking explicit operator, oracle, and slashing risk.
